Rules
  • Each player starts with a hand of 10 triangular tiles, numbered 1 to 10. One player has black tiles, the other has white tiles.
  • Players take it in turns, starting with black.
  • On each turn, the player selects one tile from their hand and places it on the board in any empty triangle.
  • Once both players have emptied their hand, there will be a single empty triangle left on the board. Black's score is the sum of the black tiles adjacent to that empty triangle, and likewise for white. The winner is whoever has the highest score, or a tie is declared if the scores are equal.
